在 TikTok 的一年：回顧與心得

<img src="pic1.png" alt="logo of TikTok" width="80" style="display: block; margin: 20px auto; border-radius: 8px; box-shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.1);">


This month marks my one-year milestone at TikTok—a chapter filled with intense learning and unexpected growth. Here are some insights that have defined my journey: <br>
這個月，我在 TikTok 工作滿一周年，這段經歷充滿緊湊的學習和意想不到的成長。以下是我旅程中收穫的深刻體悟：

• 𝘚𝘩𝘢𝘳𝘦 𝘰𝘱𝘦𝘯𝘭𝘺 𝘵𝘰 𝘨𝘳𝘰𝘸 𝘤𝘰𝘭𝘭𝘦𝘤𝘵𝘪𝘷𝘦𝘭𝘺: <br>
Inspired by several key influencers, writing LinkedIn posts taught me the power of reflection and community feedback. Documenting my ideas clarifies my own thinking, surfaces new insights from peers, and sparks meaningful discussions that accelerate everyone’s growth. <br>
• 公開分享，共同成長： <br>
受幾位重要前輩和意見領袖啟發，我在 LinkedIn 寫文章的過程，讓我深刻感受到反思和社群回饋的力量。記錄自己的想法不僅釐清思路，還能從同事身上獲得新觀點，並激發有意義的討論，加速大家的成長。

• 𝘈𝘯𝘤𝘩𝘰𝘳 𝘪𝘯 𝘤𝘦𝘳𝘵𝘢𝘪𝘯𝘵𝘺 𝘢𝘮𝘪𝘥 𝘤𝘩𝘢𝘰𝘴: <br>
In a hyper-growth environment like TikTok, ambiguity is the default. I’ve learned that true resilience lies not in avoiding uncertainty, but in creating pockets of certainty within it. The ability to find specific, actionable goals within uncertain situations—and executing them with precision—is the most critical skill I’ve honed this year. <br>
• 在混亂中穩住陣腳： <br>
在 TikTok 這樣高速成長的環境，不確定性是常態。我體會到，真正的韌性不在於逃避不確定，而是能在其中創造小範圍的確定感。今年我磨練出最重要的技能，就是在不確定情境下找到具體可行的目標，並精準執行。

• 𝘋𝘳𝘪𝘷𝘦𝘯 𝘣𝘺 𝘉𝘶𝘪𝘭𝘥𝘦𝘳'𝘴 𝘔𝘪𝘯𝘥𝘴𝘦𝘵 (𝘈𝘯𝘢𝘭𝘺𝘴𝘪𝘴 & 𝘖𝘸𝘯𝘦𝘳𝘴𝘩𝘪𝘱): <br>
I am at my best when I am building and driving, fueling my passion for ownership by deconstructing complex challenges through rigorous analysis and mapping out seamless, logical flows. There is a profound sense of achievement in constructing a solution from inception to completion—architecting robust processes that transform high-stakes uncertainty into steady, measurable progress. This is not just the work I love; it is where my analytical edge and commitment to stability deliver the highest strategic value. <br>
• 以建設者心態驅動（分析與主人翁精神）： <br>
當我在打造和推動專案時，狀態最為良好；透過嚴謹分析拆解複雜挑戰，規劃流暢的邏輯流程，讓我對專案責任更有熱情。從零開始建構解決方案直到完成，那份成就感格外深刻——設計堅實的流程，將高風險的不確定性轉化為穩健可衡量的進展。這不僅是我鍾愛的工作，更是我發揮分析優勢與追求穩定，創造最大策略價值之處。

• 𝘌𝘮𝘣𝘳𝘢𝘤𝘦 𝘵𝘳𝘢𝘯𝘴𝘧𝘦𝘳𝘢𝘣𝘭𝘦 𝘴𝘬𝘪𝘭𝘭𝘴 𝘢𝘯𝘥 𝘥𝘪𝘷𝘦𝘳𝘴𝘦 𝘣𝘢𝘤𝘬𝘨𝘳𝘰𝘶𝘯𝘥𝘴: <br>
Coming from manufacturing and supply-chain roles, I discovered many principles transfer directly to content-moderation systems and operations. For example, error-proofing concepts from the production line can be applied to review workflows to reduce human mistakes. Diverse experiences create unexpected leverage — they help you see solutions cross-functionally. <br>
• 善用可轉移技能與多元背景： <br>
我過去在製造業和供應鏈累積的經驗，讓我發現許多原則能直接套用到內容審核系統和運營上。例如，生產線的防錯機制，可應用於審核流程來減少人為疏失。多元背景帶來意想不到的優勢——它幫助你跳脫框架，跨部門洞察解決方案。

It has been a privilege to learn from such a dynamic environment. I am excited to keep bridging my past experiences with new challenges, focusing on creating stable systems and meaningful impact in the year ahead. <br>
能在如此充滿活力的環境中學習，我深感幸運。我期待持續融合過去經驗與新挑戰，在未來一年專注打造穩定系統，創造真正有意義的影響。
