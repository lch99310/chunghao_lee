Match the Interaction to the Task

Three zones, three interfaces

Sort tasks along a single axis — how clear your intent is — and the right interaction becomes derivable, not a matter of taste.
Direct Manipulation
Clear intent
+ clear object
e.g. crop a photo, change a date, adjust the volume
just point & do — no AI needed
Integration Zone ★
Speakable intent
+ visible object
e.g. "turn this table into a pie chart" · "make this paragraph more casual"
point the noun,
speak the verb
Chat Box
Fuzzy intent,
synthesis needed
e.g. "find the weaknesses in this proposal"
no object to point at — language wins
intent clear · object in viewintent fuzzy · no object
Most daily work lives in the middle — the zone the industry skipped.
