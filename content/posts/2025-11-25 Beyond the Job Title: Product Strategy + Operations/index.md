We often treat PM (Product/Program Management) and Operations as separate silos. But here’s the quiet truth: **Value isn't created in a silo.**

The ultimate metric? A user’s willingness to pay with cash or time. To truly validate that value, you must stop designing from the sidelines—**you have to be the operator.**

<br>

我們常把產品（或專案）管理（PM）與營運視為彼此隔離的孤島。但有一個少有人說破的真相：**價值從來不是在孤島中創造的。**

終極的衡量標準是什麼？就是使用者是否願意付出金錢或時間。要真正驗證這份價值，你就不能只站在旁邊設計——**你必須親自下場當營運者。**

![](pic.png)

---

## 💡 The Dual Identity: The "Berkshire Hathaway Advantage"

This is the core of the "Berkshire Hathaway Advantage." I strongly resonate with Warren Buffett’s perspective:

> *“I am a better investor because I am a businessman, and a better businessman because I am an investor.”*

This implies a powerful truth for product leaders: **Dual Identity = Dual Advantage.**

<br>

## 💡 雙重身分：「波克夏優勢」

這正是「波克夏優勢」的核心。我非常認同華倫·巴菲特的觀點：

> 「我之所以成為更好的投資人，是因為我同時是企業經營者；而我也因為身為投資人，成為更好的企業經營者。」

對產品領導者而言，這揭示了一個強而有力的真相：**擁有雙重身分，就等於擁抱雙重優勢。**

---

## 🔄 The Virtuous Cycle: Hybrid Thinking Wins

The best PMs embrace this duality. When you step out of the "designer" role and into the "operator" role, you create a necessary feedback loop:

* 🛠️ **Design informs Ops:** Knowing how the tool was built helps you maximize efficiency and output.  
* 💡 **Ops informs Design:** Feeling the pain points firsthand forces you to build **value**, not just features.

You stop building in a vacuum.

<br>

## 🔄 正向循環：跨界思維者勝出

頂尖的產品經理都擁抱這種雙重角色。當你從「設計者」走入「營運者」的身分，就自然建立起一個必要的反饋循環：

* 🛠️ **設計指引營運**：了解工具是如何建構的，能幫助你最大化效率與產出。  
* 💡 **營運反哺設計**：親身體驗痛點，才會迫使你打造真正的「價值」，而不只是堆疊功能。

你不再閉門造車。

---

## 🎯 The Strategic Takeaway: Don’t Just Architect, Inhabit

The most valuable leaders leverage operational friction to refine their strategy. The future belongs to the inhabitants, not the architects.

In short: **Don't just build the machine—operate it to find the real insights.**

<br>

## 🎯 策略啟示：別只當建築師，更要成為居住者

最有價值的領導者，懂得利用營運中的摩擦來打磨策略。未來屬於那些「親身居住其中」的人，而非僅僅畫出藍圖的建築師。

簡言之：**別只建造機器——要親自操作它，才能挖出真正的洞察。**
