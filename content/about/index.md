### Industrial AI PM bridging atom factories and token factories.
##### 工業 AI PM，橫跨原子工廠與詞元工廠。

Hi, I'm **Chung-Hao (CH) Lee 李崇豪** — based in Sydney.

I started on factory floors and grew into AI systems. At **Pegatron Suzhou**, I ran a 15-person team operating an AIoT manufacturing line producing 2M units per year. At **Wistron Taipei** — a global Tier-1 server ODM (Dell, HPE) — I delivered **A$1.2M in annual cost savings** through AI-driven predictive maintenance across **40B+ sensor records**, and deployed GPT-4 for log analysis at scale. Now at **TikTok Sydney**, I operate **35 AI moderation models serving 1B+ users and 12M+ weekly content**, and built the **RCA Copilot** human-AI collaboration platform from 0 to 1.

我從工廠車間出發，一路走到 AI 系統。在**和碩蘇州**，我帶 15 人團隊運營年產 200 萬台的 AIoT 製造線；在**緯創台北**（Dell、HPE 等全球 Tier-1 伺服器 ODM 廠），我透過 AI 驅動的預測性維護創造 **A$1.2M 的年度成本節省**，分析 **400 億筆感測器資料**，並在產線上部署 GPT-4 進行 log 分析。目前在 **TikTok 雪梨**，我運營 **35 個 AI 審核模型，服務 10 億+ 使用者、每週 1,200 萬+ 內容**，並從 0 到 1 建立 **RCA Copilot 人機協作平台**。

What carries across these roles isn't a domain — it's a method I've come to call **"Analysis + Process"**: deconstructing ambiguous problems into measurable parts (analysis), then designing repeatable workflows that close the loop (process). I wrote about [why these two keywords define my work](/posts/the-two-keywords-analysis-and-process/) — the Wistron "No Power recall plunge" case is the canonical example.

橫跨這些角色，貫穿始終的不是某個領域，而是一套我稱為**「分析 + 流程」**的工作方法：把模糊的問題拆解成可量測的要素（分析），再設計能閉環的可重複流程（流程）。我寫過 [這兩個關鍵字如何定義我至今的職涯](/posts/the-two-keywords-analysis-and-process/)，緯創時期的「No Power 召回率驟降」就是最具代表性的案例。
